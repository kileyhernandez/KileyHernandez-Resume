Kiley Hernandez

Dance

I began dancing in eigth grade for the high school dance team. I served as a junior captain my sophomore year, captain my junior and senior year, and eventual choreographer for the team when I graduated. I used my artistic capabilitie to make intriguing and beautiful solos, trios, duos, and group dances.

In college, I minored in the art of dance. I completed classes on the history of dance, the cultural aspects, the issues surrounding the dance field, and movement classes in all genres of dance. In college I gained an enriched education of all aspects of dance.

In graduate school I assisted renowned professors of dance. Travelling to Europe, New York, Oregon, and Asia, my knowledge of dance expanded. I am grateful to have acquired knowledge that I can apply throughout my teaching and life overall.
